About Us

You have plenty of choices when it comes to getting a haircut in Ottawa, but only Marion Barbershop can offer you over 40 years of experience, old-school barbery techniques and an elevated experience in and out of the chair.

Marion Barbershop is a local, Ottawa-based barbershop located in the heart of the Byward Market. Specializing in haircuts, beard trimming and shaves for discerning gentlemen of all ages, Marion Barbershop takes pride in providing you with the best hairstyle, shave or beard trim to make you look and feel your best.
